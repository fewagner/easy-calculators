Qubit–Feedline T₁ Estimator

The mutual capacitance Cmf and the island-to-ground capacitance Cgnd are linearly interpolated from a lookup table computed with a boundary-element method (BEM). Simulation parameters: half silicon / half vacuum substrate (εeff = (1 + εSi)/2 ≈ 6.2), feedline length 1.98 mm, boundary box 2 mm × 2 mm, feedline centre-conductor width 10 μm, gap to either side 5.5 μm. The table covers R ∈ {50, 100, 200} μm, D ∈ {100, 200, 400} μm, s ∈ {5, 10, 20} μm; values are linearly interpolated between grid points. T₁ is then calculated using input-output theory.

⚠ Parameter combination is outside or near the edge of the BEM grid — result involves extrapolation and may be less accurate.
