Formulas & model

QuantityFormula / Note
Coherence length ξ ξ = √(ℏD / (2πkBTc))
Effective Tc (Cooper limit) Tc,eff = exp[(d1 ln Tc,1 + d2 ln Tc,2) / (d1 + d2)]
Gap estimate Δ(0) ≈ 1.76 kB Tc,eff  (BCS)
Regime (Cooper limit) d < 0.3 ξ for both layers → uniform proximitized bilayer
Regime (coupled) d < ξ for both layers → coupled but non-uniform gap
Thick film regime d ≥ ξ for either layer → strong spatial variation, Cooper limit invalid

Note: The Cooper-limit formula assumes equal density of states (thickness-weighted average). It is most accurate when both layers are thin compared to their coherence lengths. For thick films the result is an overestimate and a full Usadel numerical solution is required.
